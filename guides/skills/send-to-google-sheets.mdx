Anagram can send information from a conversation to Google Sheets. You can use this to capture contact information from sales leads, create dynamic polls and surveys, and gather information from any type of experience.

## **Before you start: Connecting the Google Sheets integration**

Before enabling the skill, first navigate to the **Data Hub** and connect the **Google Sheets integration**. You'll be asked to authenticate with your Google account and accept the permissions to set it up.
